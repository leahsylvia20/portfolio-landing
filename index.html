About me.

Hello there! I’m Leah, an Epicodus student. I have very little coding experience, but use WordPress here and there in my current role as a Marketing and Outreach Coordinator for a local nonprofit. I studied graphic design and Community and Environmental Sociology at UW-Madison. I decided to enroll at Epicodus to see if delving into the web programming world would be a good career choice for me. I’m excited to be learning and hope to acquire the general knowledge and understanding of various web coding languages to take with me into my next job. Some of my interests outside of coding include hiking, backpacking, cooking, baking, reading, photography, walking my dog, and traveling. I’m looking forward to traveling around the world next year with my fiancé on sabbatical. I hope to continue to expand my web programming skills while traveling and hope to find some freelance work along the way.
